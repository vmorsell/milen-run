Spring 2022 - Rize of the Fenix

Post-GD parties are back. Study sessions in KTH Library are back. Damn, even Bullens hangouts after 23.00 are back!

Now it's time for Milen med Mörsell to rise like the mighty Fenix. It's outside, covid friendly (no more Spex Pong Cup spreading, please) and good for body and mind.

Long-sleeve clothing is recommended. Don't forget to bring a warm sweater to put on after the run.

What is this?

Milen med Mörsell is a running activity for students in iSpexet at KTH and other cool cats. The concept is as simple as it is pleasant. Run 10 km along with a sweet group of peers after the exam period and get a dinner. We run one lap together around Kungsholmen in slow pace.

Everyone is welcome, no previous experience of long distance running necessary.

Due to covid-19 the dinner will be to-go. The number of servings is limited and first come, first served applies.

When?

Sunday, 20 March. Rendez-vous 14.00 at Bergsgatan 51 to drop off bags, clothes etc. Closest subway station: Fridhemsplan or Rådhuset.
