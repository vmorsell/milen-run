Summer 2022 Edition

Post-GD parties are back. The Porter Festival is back back. Damn, even hangouts at Bullens after 23.00 is back!

It's time for Milen med Mörsell to rise one last time like the mighty Fenix. It's outdoors and good for body and mind.

What is this?

Milen med Mörsell is a running activity for students in iSpexet at KTH and other cool cats. The concept is as simple as it is pleasant. Run 10 km along with a sweet group of peers after the exam period and get a dinner. We run one lap together around Kungsholmen at a slow pace.

Everyone is welcome, no previous experience of long distance running necessary.

After the run we go to Rålis for some sun (or water) bath as well as to get the nutritions your bodies so desperately crave. Non-runners are of course welcome to join as well!

When?

Date to be decided. Rendez-vous 14.00 at Bergsgatan 51 to drop off bags and clothes. Closest subway stations are Rådhuset and Fridhemsplan.
